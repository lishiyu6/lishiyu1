Brand Introduction

SHIYU Handmake Studio is an independent studio focusing on hand-knitting, founded by designer Shiyu. With exquisite handicraft skills and unique design concepts, the brand creates exquisite hand-knitted clothing for doll collectors around the world. Each work condenses the craftsman's painstaking efforts and temperature, and is committed to providing a unique miniature fashion experience for dolls. Brand products cover seasonal limited series, classic replicas and customized services to meet the personalized needs of different collectors. We insist on using high-quality natural materials and handmade to ensure that each piece of work is not only beautiful, but also durable.

Brand promise

• Pure handmade: rejecting assembly line production, every work is unique.                                                                                                      • Environmental sustainability: From material selection to packaging design, it follows the concept of environmental protection.                    Let's weave your fairy tales with yarn!

Brand Vision

We believe that every handicraft is not only a commodity, but also an emotional carrier. Through unique design and exquisite craftsmanship, we hope that each collector can establish a deep emotional connection with their dolls. Whether as a gift or a personal collection, these works can evoke good memories and emotional resonance. Let every doll become a partner in your life and accompany you through every important moment.

Brand Mission

SHIYU Handmake Studio is committed to inspiring the creativity and imagination of doll collectors. We provide personalized customization services, allowing customers to according to their own preferences and style. Choose fabrics, colors and design elements to make the costumes of each doll unique. Our goal is to give each doll a unique life and personality through unique design and exquisite hand-made. Let them not only be static toys, but also works of art rich in stories and emotions.
